There are 5 tpes of positions. They are 

        i> Static position : by default
        ii> Relativa position : move according to its own original position
        iii> Absolute position : moves according to its parent. If parents is not mentioned it moves according to body.
        iv> Fixed position : moves according to body and have its fixed define position
        v> Sticky position : moves according to body and stick after the define position meets
